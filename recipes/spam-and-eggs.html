Spam and Eggs

Did you just say Spam?

When some people hear the dreaded four letter word Spam, they're minds think of something inedible.

These people could not be more wrong because Spam (and eggs) is not only quick and easy, but incredibly delicious.

If you made it this far and want to see what the hype is all about, keep reading.

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 ounce) container: fully cooked luncheon meat (e.g. Spam), cubed
  • 2: eggs beaten
  • 2 ounces: cheddar cheese, grated

Steps

  1. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  2. Pour eggs, then Spam.
  3. Cook, stirring, until eggs are nearly done, then sprinkle cheese over, and stir until melted.
